Story of the game:

The evil clouds have stolen all of your Ninja village's coins. Take back as many as you can!

Purpose of the game:

The purpose of the game is to collect as many coins as possible and survive as long as possible. You can't win, but you can pretend to win if you have the highest score.
You lose if you fall off the platforms.

Controls of the game:

  • Left Arrow - Move left

  • Right Arrow - Move right

  • Down Arrow - Stop jump and start falling if held

  • Space bar - Jump
